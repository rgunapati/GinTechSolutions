About

20 Years Building Enterprise Data & AI Platforms

GinTech Solutions is an Enterprise Data & AI consulting firm with 20+ years of deep, hands-on experience designing, implementing, and governing large-scale data and AI platforms across multi-cloud environments.

Our expertise spans the full arc of modern data — from large-scale SAP BW/HANA implementations for Fortune 500 companies in healthcare, manufacturing, and energy, to enterprise data platform modernization on Snowflake and Azure, to architecting data infrastructure on Snowflake AI Data Cloud in AWS GovCloud for federal agencies, including delivering GenAI proof-of-concepts using Snowflake Cortex.

GinTech Solutions brings certified expertise across Snowflake (SnowPro Core & GenAI Specialty), AWS, Azure, and PMP — selectively taking on strategic, high-impact data and AI architecture engagements for organizations that are serious about doing data right.
